About

LTRJ labs, pronounced 'letter J labs,' is a tech startup in Victoria, BC founded by four electrical engineering students with a desire for a platform to let our ideas and skills come to life.

We started this company to bring our skills to industry in a confident and ambitious fashion. Our mission is to provide affordable and professional design services for businesses and hobbyists. We are all at the tail end of our Electronics Engineering Technology diploma, with only a capstone semester left. LTRJ Labs will be fully operational as of May 10th, 2026. We are hopeful that you will join us on this exciting journey! We have a lot to learn and are open to all opportunities you may have in store for us.
